Aberfan and other Shorthand stories

For the 50th anniversary of the Aberfan disaster, WalesOnline wanted to do something special to remember the tragedy. In addition to publishing lots of stories on the main website, we decided to make a longform story that would serve as a lasting digital memorial where the people who survived the disaster and whose lives are forever changed told their stories.
